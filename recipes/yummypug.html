Yum Yum Sweaty Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 12 Sweet Potatoes
  • Treadmill
  • Boxing Gloves (optional)

Steps

  1. Buy Potatoes
  2. Put Potatoes on Treadmill
  3. Punch Potatoes
  4. Serve Warm

What you want to do is take the sweet potato and put it on a treadmill. Make that potato work. Once you've got it good and sweaty the real fun begins.

Leave the sweat on the sweaty potato. Make sure that baby is dripping. Then punch that baby in the face. After that she should be good and docile. Serve warm.
